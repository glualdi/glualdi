About Us

What is ScribeAR?

Designed with heads-up display technology in mind, ScribeAR is live transcription software that enables users of real-time captioning services to simultaneously maintain visual access to their environment. Integrated with automated speech recognition technologies, ScribeAR eliminates the need for expensive, third-party captioning services. A lightweight live-transcription platform designed with HTML5 to be platform agnostic, ScribeAR can be used on multiple devices and AR headsets.

Features

Any platform, any service, anywhere.

Whether you’re choosing between software or hardware, ScribeAR meets you wherever you are. Conventional captioning? Augmented Reality capability? Universally accessible from any phone, tablet, or computer? Check, check, and check.

Native integration makes choosing easy.

With so many different captioning services out there, it can be overwhelming to have to try and find the right one. From Azure to WebSpeech, ScribeAR keeps all your captioning engines in one convenient place.

The little things that aren't so little.

Sound vizualization. Caption font customization. Edit and share tools. With so many ways to interact and personalize, ScribeAR makes your captions feel like your captions.
